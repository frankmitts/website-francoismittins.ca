WHY "STAGE"?

//Definition: A period or step in a process, activity, or development.

As artists, we're continually evolving our work. Not satisfied to remain static, we explore and experiment. I believe we move through developmental cycles, much like the stages of human development. That's why every STAGE I strive to push my creativity and boundaries to new levels.

STAGE 01

Withdrawn from reality, she immersed in a dream of flashing lights and designer fabrics. Forever reaching her goals and overcoming life's challenges.

SANCTUM

A place where one is free from intrusion. A sacred place to channel your creative mind.

RAWNESS

In a society where expressing our true selves is becoming harder and harder, RAWNESS is needed. It’s required in our emotions and who we are. To fully express ourselves, we need to embrace our souls first. Embrace our light as much as our darkness.

MOMENT

"My most important message to all of you is to be kind or be quiet. We know sticks and stones break our bones, but words pierce and stay engraved in the mind and heart longer than most physical wounds. At the end of the day, we are ALL human, wanting nothing more than to be loved and accepted for exactly who we are. Hand in hand, heart to heart, we’re stronger when we aren’t ripping each other apart.”

LE PETIT DÉJEUNER

Morning. The irony of a beginning that starts with an end. A whole world in panic while routine breakfast and thoughts are here to see the day go by. Calm within chaos and purity within confinement.
